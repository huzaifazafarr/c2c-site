Who We Are

Bridging the Gap Between Education and Employment

Campus to Corporate (C2C) is a student-founded, community-driven career preparation platform born at IBA Karachi. We exist because we experienced the same problem ourselves — navigating internship season without proper guidance is overwhelming.

We connect students with real HR professionals, management trainees, and industry mentors through a structured platform that is affordable, local, and deeply understood in the Pakistani corporate context.

Pakistan-specific guidance Direct recruiter access Student-friendly pricing IBA-founded & student-run
The Problem

Pakistan graduates hundreds of thousands of students yearly who enter the job market unprepared for what recruiters actually look for.

Our Solution

Structured, affordable career services — CV reviews and 1-on-1 counselling — backed by professionals who understand local hiring.

Our Vision

To become Pakistan's most trusted career preparation platform, scaling from IBA Karachi to every major university across the country.

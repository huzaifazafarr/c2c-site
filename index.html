Who We Are

Bridging Education and Employment

C2C is a student-founded career preparation platform built at IBA Karachi. We connect students with real HR professionals, management trainees, and industry mentors through structured, affordable services rooted in the Pakistani corporate context.

Our Vision

To become Pakistan's most trusted career preparation platform, expanding from IBA Karachi to every major university across the country.
